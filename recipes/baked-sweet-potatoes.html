Baked Sweet Potatoes

These baked sweet potato cubes will pair perfectly with all your favorite seasonal dinners.

This is a very good and healthy recipe

Ingrediants

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 pinches dried oregano or to taste
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingrediants
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) coat the bottom of a glass or nonstick baking dish with olive oil, just enough to coat.
  3. Wash and peel sweet potatoes, cut into medium pieces.
  4. Place in the baking dish and stir to coat with olive oil. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until soft and fork-tender, about 45 minuts to 1 hour.
  6. Enjoy
